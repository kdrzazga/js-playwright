Welcome to the world of electric wires!
You need to provide electricity to building floors. Sounds easy? Not exactly, since animals like rats, bats and spiders can make your job a nightmare.

You are standing on a floor ceiling. See that wire endings on left and right? Simply draw a wire between them to advance to level 1:

Good luck!

-------------

The game is a loose adaptation of 8-bit Atari game published with the same title in 1984 by Synapse Software.
